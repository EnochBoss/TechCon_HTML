Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 by a group of technology enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform for knowledge sharing in the rapidly evolving tech industry. What began as a small gathering of 200 attendees has now grown into one of the premier tech conferences worldwide, hosting over 10,000 participants annually.

Over the years, TechCon has been hosted in various tech hubs including San Francisco, Berlin, and Tokyo, bringing together the brightest minds in technology.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to foster innovation through education, collaboration, and networking. We believe technology should be accessible to all and aim to:

  • Bridge the gap between academia and industry
  • Promote diversity in tech
  • Showcase cutting-edge technological advancements
  • Create opportunities for meaningful connections
